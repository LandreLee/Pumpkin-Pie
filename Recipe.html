The best Pumpkin Pie you will ever have for Thanksgiving!

What you will need.

-1ea small pumpkin (1 1/3 cup)

-1 can sweetened condensed milk (1 1/3 cup)

-1ea egg

-Cinnamon (1 1/4 teaspoon)

-Nutmeg (1/2 teaspoon)

-Ground Ginger (1/2 teaspoon)

-Cloves (1/4 teaspoon)

-Hot Water (1 cup)

-1ea 9-inch pie shell (I prefer the graham cracker crust from the grocery store, pre-made)

Step by step instructions

1.) Cut pumpkin in half and remove all seeds and strings. Place face down on a baking sheet lined with foil. Cover with foil and bake at 350 degrees for 1 1/2 hours.

2.) Let pumpkin cool and using a spoon gently remove any excess strings. Take same spoon and scoop out the flesh of the pumpkin into a separate container. Mash pumpkin and drain, if too watery. Pumpkin in this state can stay good up to 3 days refrigerated.

3.) Pre-heat oven to 375 degrees.

4.) Mix all your ingredients into a bowl until smooth.

5.) Place into the 9-inch pie shell and carefully insert into pre-heated oven.

6.) Bake at 375 degrees for 55-65 minutes.

7.) Enjoy!
